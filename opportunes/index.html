Surface, then decide

Each card is an opportune. AI ranks all four channels against each other into your Top Opportunes, then explains why each one matters and drafts a first move. Every step stays editable. Nothing ships without you.

The human is the decider, by design

Opportunes ranks; it never acts on your behalf. Pass is a first-class action, not a hidden one. "Not Me Yet" sits more prominently than Publish, because most of what AI surfaces is worth declining, and declining well is the skill.
