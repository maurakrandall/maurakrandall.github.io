## The loop

This repository is the portfolio itself, organized by the Human–AI Loop: **Test → Build → Codify → Share**. The structure is the argument. Each stage points to working artifacts you can open.

### Test — probing ideas with real prototypes
- **Opportunes:** a personal opportunity OS on a multi-agent workflow. Human decides, AI ranks.

### Build — real products people can use
- **Team Context Builder:** methodology becoming product. Context is collaboration infrastructure. *(flagship)*
- **Daily Fuel:** an AI-native nutrition companion for life on GLP-1. [Live](https://dailyfuel.onrender.com).
- **BurnoutBuddy:** a reflective companion for navigating burnout with clarity. *(in build)*

### Codify — the methodology, made canonical and demonstrated
- [The Human–AI Loop](https://thehumanailoop.com): the published methodology
- [The Loop Simulator](https://try.thehumanailoop.com): the method in motion
- [Triad in Slack](https://maurakrandall.github.io/triad-in-slack/): AI teammates in a shared workspace
- [Strategist as Conductor](https://maurakrandall.github.io/delivery-loop-scene/): one human conducting AI through a live decision

### Share — teaching the practice in public
- [AI Literacy Library](https://aigal.io/ai-literacy/): frameworks and coined terms
- [Triad in Slack — Infographic](https://maurakrandall.github.io/triad-in-slack-infographic/): the methodology at a glance
- [Writing & Reflections](https://maurakrandall.substack.com): the philosophy series

## The Triad

Every product here was built by one human directing two AI collaborators, each with a distinct role:

- **Maura:** product vision and the final call
- **CP** (ChatGPT): divergent thinking and rapid prototyping
- **Soph** (Claude): synthesis and documentation

Maura carries the context and makes the decisions. The AI teammates contribute differently, and visibly. The work is better for the division of labor.
